Smart Circuit Breaker

This project focuses on developing a smart circuit breaker designed to ensure the safety of linemen or maintenance teams. The system utilizes a 4x4 matrix keypad for user input and control, while incorporating light bulbs to represent the load or machinery being managed. The circuit breaker aims to provide enhanced protection by enabling precise control and real-time monitoring, ultimately contributing to a safer working environment for electrical maintenance tasks..
